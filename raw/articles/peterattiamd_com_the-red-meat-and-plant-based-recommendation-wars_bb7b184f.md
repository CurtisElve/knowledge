meat[1](javascript:void(0))“Red meat was defined as mammalian meat, and processed meat was defined

cardiovascular disease, cancer, and all-cause mortality.

This group of investigators ultimately recommended that adults continue eating the current levels of
red and processed meat while acknowledging that this was a “weak recommendation,” based on evidence
Much of that evidence is in the form of observational studies, and are limited because of
confounding (e.g., the healthy user bias), lack of accuracy in design (e.g., the use of unreliable
food frequency questionnaires), and because they inherently cannot determine causation.
